Topic: Evolution of Humans (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/santiago123678/evolution-of-humans-datasets-for-clasification)

Project description: With 12,000 rows and 28 columns, this dataset covers a 
wide range of traits of different hominids, from the earliest compatible 
ancestors to modern Homo sapiens. The aim is to predict gender and 
species or whether or not they were bipedal.
